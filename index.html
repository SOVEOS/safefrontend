SAFE Token Rules and Information

SAFE is a new stablecoin that is fully collateralized by USDT, and similar to REX, can only increase in value.

The SAFE smart-contract is open-source, decentralized, and immutable. It has no keys and can only be ammended with a 15/21 producing BP consensus. SAFE combining these features gives it the highest level of security available on EOSIO.

SAFE is minted by sending USDT to the SAFE contract. There is a 5% fee for minting SAFE. 1% will be used to automatically buyback and burn SVX and 4% goes to all SAFE holders equally and instantly. This is done by using the fee to increase the amount of USDT each SAFE is collateralized by, and redeemable for whenever SAFE is minted.

When minting SAFE, the newly minted tokens are also instantly increased in value. Because of this, you always earn back part of your minting fee and it is possible to pay a minting fee as low as 1% (depending on the current SAFE supply and the amount that you mint).

SAFE is converted back to USDT by sending it to the SAFE smart-contract. The SAFE smart-contract will then destroy the received SAFE tokens and send the value in USDT to your account. There are no fees to convert SAFE to USDT.

SAFE is the official stable coin of SOVDEX and will be tradeable in additional markets offering yield farming soon. SAFE minters will also mine SVX as a bonus. The reward is dynamic and will increase and decrease according to minting activity.
